  • Research

    Research

    Building a robot-prosthesis for people without a hand. The idea of this prosthesis relies on sensors which are reading electro magnetic impulses from the muscles + engines making prosthetics squeeze and unsqueeze + microcomputer (to serve signals from sensors and push signal to engines) + power bank + 3d printed prosthetics (or delivered) + neural network + camera to confirm the behavioral pattern.

    Participating in a research with finding medicine against epilepsy and my role is to analyze experiments with mice, collect their trajectories of moving to prove the effect of some substances.
